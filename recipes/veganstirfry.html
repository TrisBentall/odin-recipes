Vegan Stir Fry

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Serves: Serves 4

The BEST vegan stir fry is made with lots of fresh veggies, tofu and a sweet and savory stir fry sauce to tantalize your tastebuds!

You can mix and match the veggies so it's very customizable to what you have on hand. Perfect for when you have vegetables in the fridge that you need to use.

Ingredients

For the Stir Fry:

  • 450g extra firm tofu
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 200g sugar snap peas
  • 4 medium bell peppers
  • 240g chestnut mushrooms
  • 200g baby corn
  • 1/2 cup spring onions

For the Stir Fry Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Press the tofu for 30 minutes. It's ideal if you have a tofu press, but if you don't then place the tofu onto a plate with another plate on top and then place something heavy on top of that like a heavy pot. Let it press for 30 minutes to get rid of as much water as possible.
  2. While the tofu is pressing prepare your veggies and mix up your stir fry sauce by adding all the sauce ingredients to a measuring jug and whisking together until mixed.
  3. When the tofu has finished pressing, cut it into cubes.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of sesame oil to a wok and heat it on medium heat. When it's hot add the tofu cubes and fry them until the tofu is golden brown. Then remove the tofu from the wok.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of sesame oil to the wok and add chopped sugar snap peas, bell peppers, mushrooms and baby corn and stir fry for a few minutes until the veggies are soft but still firm.
  6. Add tofu and chopped spring onions and pour over the sauce. Stir fry for a couple of minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  7. Serve over rice, topped with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and chopped spring onions.
